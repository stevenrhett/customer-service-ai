# Pricing and Billing Information

### Basic Plan - $29/month
- Up to 10 users
- 5 GB storage per user
- Email support
- 99.9% uptime SLA
- Monthly billing cycle
- No setup fee

### Professional Plan - $79/month
- Up to 50 users
- 25 GB storage per user
- Priority email & chat support

- Unlimited users

## Billing Cycles

All plans are billed on a monthly basis by default. The billing date is set when you first subscribe and remains consistent each month. For example, if you subscribe on the 15th, you'll be billed on the 15th of each subsequent month.

Annual billing is available for Professional and Enterprise plans with a 10% discount applied to the total annual cost.

## Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
- Debit cards
- PayPal
- Wire transfer (Enterprise plan only, minimum annual contract)
- ACH transfer (Enterprise plan only)

## Refund Policy

### 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
We offer a full refund within 30 days of your initial subscription to any plan. To request a refund, contact our billing support team at billing@company.com.

### Pro-rated Refunds
If you downgrade or cancel after 30 days, we do not offer pro-rated refunds for the current billing period. However, you will retain access to your plan features until the end of your current billing cycle.

### Disputed Charges
If you believe you've been charged incorrectly, please contact our billing team within 60 days of the charge. We'll investigate and issue a refund if an error occurred.

## Invoicing

### Invoice Delivery
Invoices are automatically sent to the billing email address on file within 24 hours of each billing cycle. You can also download invoices at any time from your account dashboard under "Billing > Invoices."

### Invoice Details
Each invoice includes:
- Invoice number (format: INV-YYYY-MM-XXXXX)
- Billing period
- Itemized charges
- Total amount due
- Payment method used
- Transaction ID

### Past Invoices
You can access up to 24 months of past invoices in your account dashboard. For older invoices, contact billing@company.com.

## Late Payments and Account Suspension

### Grace Period
If a payment fails, we'll retry the charge three times over a 7-day period. You'll receive email notifications for each failed attempt.

### Account Suspension
If payment is not received after 7 days, your account will be suspended. During suspension:
- You cannot access your data
- No new data can be added
- Users cannot log in
- You have 30 days to resolve payment issues

### Account Termination
If payment is not received within 30 days of suspension, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted. We send reminder emails at days 14, 21, and 28 of the suspension period.

## Upgrades and Downgrades

### Upgrading Your Plan
You can upgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and you'll be charged a pro-rated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle at the new plan rate.

### Downgrading Your Plan
Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. You'll continue to have access to your current plan features until then.

### User Seat Adjustments
You can add or remove user seats at any time:
- Additional seats are charged pro-rata for the current billing period
- Removed seats result in a credit applied to your next invoice
- Minimum seat requirements apply per plan tier

## Tax Information

### Sales Tax
Sales tax is calculated based on your billing address and applicable local regulations. Tax rates are clearly shown on your invoice.

### VAT (European Customers)
EU customers are charged VAT based on their country of residence. If you provide a valid VAT ID, reverse charge may apply.

### Tax Exemption
If your organization is tax-exempt, please provide your tax exemption certificate to billing@company.com. We'll update your account within 3-5 business days.

## Cancellation Policy

### How to Cancel
To cancel your subscription:
1. Log into your account
2. Navigate to Billing > Subscription
3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
4. Follow the confirmation prompts

Alternatively, contact our support team at support@company.com.

### When Cancellation Takes Effect
Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You'll retain full access until that date.

### Data Export
Before cancellation, we recommend exporting your data. After cancellation, data is retained for 30 days, then permanently deleted.
